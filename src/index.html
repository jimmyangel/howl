About this site

Welcome to HOWL (Highlighting Oregon's WildLands), an interactive 3D/4D mapping and visualization tool for presenting information about the preservation of wilderness and wildlife protection in Oregon.

Data sources

  • Wildfire data from Monitoring Trends in Burn Severity (MTBS)
  • Oregon's Bureau of Land Management (BLM) old growth forests, clearcuts in Federal lands, and current/potential wilderness areas from Oregon Wild
  • Oregon ecoregions data from the United States Environmental Protection Agency (EPA)
  • Douglas complex fire photograph from the Oregon Department of Forestry
  • Badger Creek Wilderness Area photograph from Wikipedia user Axcordion
  • OR-7 wolf photograph from the Oregon Department of Fish and Wildlife

Open source components

  • Cesium Javascript library for globe and 3D mapping
  • Bootstrap for mobile friendly navigation bar and dialogs
  • jQuery mobile-friendly Javascript utility library
  • Chart.js charting library
  • Masonry cascading grid layout library
This is a work in progress. Please report bugs and omissions. We are continually testing and enhancing this application over time. You are welcome to fork this open source project and contribute to its development.

Help

To pan the view in the map area

  • Left-click and drag, or
  • Command-click and drag, or
  • Single finger drag gesture

To zoom the view in the map area

  • Right-click and drag, or
  • Mouse wheel scroll, or
  • Two finger pinch gesture

To rotate the view in the map area

  • Middle-click and drag, or
  • Control-click and drag, or
  • Two finger rotate gesture

Use the right side HOWL view panel to

  • See view legend
  • Select display options
  • Control timeline animation playback when available
